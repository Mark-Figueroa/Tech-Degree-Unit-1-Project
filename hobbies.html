Main sources of inspiration


I love reading non-fiction, trying (and cooking) new food and generally just being involved in social activites, but my biggest inspiration is and has always been GREAT video games.

I grew up writing and drawing because of the amazing content in some of my favorite video game titles. I also enlisted in the Marine Corps to make a difference in the world because of these games.

Like good art or a new experience, a good game challenges and changes the observer's perspective. These are some of the titles that lead to me living and pursuing my passions and help people however I can, regardless of the obstacles in my path:


Game Description

The Legend of Zelda Series

Each game in The Legend of Zelda series tells an important part of the history of Hyrule. It's a great series for anyone looking for a good story or an exciting adventure. Click here to learn more.

Chrono Trigger

CHRONO TRIGGER is the timeless role-playing classic developed by the ‘Dream Team’ of Dragon Quest creator Yuji Horii, Dragon Ball creator Akira Toriyama, and the creators of Final Fantasy.

As the story unfolds, embark on a journey to different eras: the present, the middle ages, the future, prehistory, and ancient times! Whether you're a first-time player or a longtime fan, this epic quest to save a planet's future promises hours of enthralling adventure! Click here to learn more.

Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Nothing I can say will accurately describe the profound magnitude of this wonderful peice of art and creativity. Whether you are new to video games or well-versed in good ol' fashioned "vidya," this is a captivating masterpiece that you can easily lose yourself in. Click here to learn more.

Pokemon Platinum

I am a huge fan of the franchise and was introduced to handheld video games through this series. None of the titles captured my attention more than Pokemon Platinum. The moral and ethical dilemmas presented pixel-after-pixel in this action-packed, gripping RPG will have you playing for hours to save the world. Click here to learn more.

Fallout 3 and Fallout: New Vegas

War. War never changes...

These are amazing games in their own respects. Both games caught in different points of my life and have had a profpund effect on the way I go about gathering information and conducting research. I won't provide any links, as these are games that aren't for everyone.
